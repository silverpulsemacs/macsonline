Hey there!

Silver Pulse is all about taking those beautiful "silver" era Macs and making them feel brand new again. We believe that just because a computer has a few years on it, doesn't mean it belongs in the bin.

We're a small, friendly lab based in Byford. We spend our nights tinkering with thermal paste, SSD upgrades, and software patches so that you can get a premium Apple experience without the $3,000 price tag. We treat every machine like it's our own, ensuring it's clean, fast, and ready to go for school or work.
